# Veridian

> AI exam marker for Pearson Edexcel IAL — Economics & Business Studies

Veridian gives level-based, examiner-accurate feedback on IAL exam answers.
It marks to official AO weightings, identifies exactly what was lost and why,
and produces a rewrite plan to move from B to A to A*.
